All About Dr. O'Grady

Before BECA

I grew up in CT, went to college in Boston and NYC. I majored in Engineering and eventually went into teaching. I have only taught in schools in Harlem and the Bronx.

During BECA

This is my 11th year of teaching at BECA. I used to teach math but moved into teaching Coding and Web Design. I also coach the school's robotics team.

After BECA

I will retire at age 55 after teaching for 27 years. My wife and I will travel and relax and work with rescuing pitbulls.
